History: Sustainajewelry was founded in 2017 by Shelly Lancaster as a way to reduce the harm humans have done on the amazon forest by planting a tree for every purchase! It is a relatively new business, but it has already started to gain a following and has created 260 hectares of new forest! Why Use Us: We give back to the planet and produce great quality jewelry!
